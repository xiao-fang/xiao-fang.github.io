<blockquote class="blockquote-center" >
Trust your heart if the seas catch fire,
live by love though the stars walk backward.
<small>E.E. Cummings</small>

若火生海面,请相信你的心;
尽管星辰陨落, 仍以爱为生.
</blockquote>
